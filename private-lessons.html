Private dance lessons.

The most personalized and effective way to learn — whether you're starting from zero, polishing a specific skill, or preparing for an event. Salsa, Bachata, Latin Hustle, Cha-Cha, and wedding choreography.

A private dance lesson is a one-on-one (or one-on-couple) session with a dedicated instructor, tailored exclusively to your needs and pace. Unlike a group class, every minute is built around you — immediate feedback, deep dives into technique, and a curriculum designed for the specific goals you walk in with. Most students progress 2–3× faster than they would in groups alone.
