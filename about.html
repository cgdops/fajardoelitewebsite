About Fajardo Elite Dance Academy

At Fajardo Elite Intl. Dance Academy, we believe that dance is a universal language, a powerful art form, and a transformative journey. Under the visionary leadership of world-renowned instructors, we offer an unparalleled dance education experience for enthusiasts of all levels.

Our Founders
Alex & Sue Romero

Fajardo Elite Intl. Dance Academy was brought to life through the vision and dedication of Alexis Romero and Sue Romero. Their commitment to creating a space where dancers could thrive, learn from the best, and share their passion laid the groundwork for what our academy is today. Their founding principles continue to inspire our mission to provide world-class dance instruction in a supportive and enriching environment.

Our Expert Leadership & Instruction

What sets Fajardo Elite apart is our commitment to excellence, embodied by our exceptional faculty.

Director

Billy Fajardo

Our academy's instructional program is proudly led by Billy Fajardo, widely recognized as the "Godfather of Latin Hustle." As a multi-time world champion and an icon in the dance world, Billy brings a lifetime of passion, expertise, and a proven track record of creating champions. His innovative techniques and profound understanding of movement are at the heart of our curriculum.

Assistant Director

Leah Duque

Complementing our leadership is Leah Duque, our esteemed Assistant Studio Director. With extensive professional dance training and a string of championship titles to her name, Leah brings a dynamic blend of grace, precision, and dedicated instruction to our students. Together, Billy and Leah cultivate an environment where potential is nurtured and talent flourishes.

About Fajardo Elite Dance Academy

Tailored Training for Every Dancer

Whether you're taking your very first steps onto the dance floor or honing your skills for a professional career, Fajardo Elite Intl. Dance Academy is your destination for world-class instruction. We specialize in:

Adult Dance Enthusiasts: Discover the joy of movement, learn new rhythms, and build confidence in our welcoming environment.

Professional Dancer Training: Elevate your artistry and technique with advanced coaching from industry leaders.

Private 1-on-1 Instruction: Benefit from highly personalized coaching designed to accelerate your progress and address your specific goals.

While private instruction is our specialty, we also offer engaging group classes for those who enjoy learning in a collaborative setting.

Join us at Fajardo Elite Intl. Dance Academy and experience the difference that truly elite instruction can make in your dance journey.
