Why Couples Choose Us

More than memorizing steps.

Learning to dance for your wedding is a fun and romantic experience you'll share before the big day. Our specialized wedding dance lessons offer:

  • Custom choreography to YOUR song — not a template, not a recital piece. We build to the music you've chosen.
  • Tailored to your skill level — whether either of you has danced before or never crossed a dance floor
  • Patient, experienced instructors — Billy, Alex & Sue, and our senior team have prepared hundreds of South Florida couples
  • Rehearsal that fits your timeline — most couples start 6–12 weeks out; we'll work with whatever timing you have
  • Coordination with your venue + DJ — entrances, exits, song edits — we'll help you think through the whole moment
  • Two left feet welcome — most of our wedding couples were nervous beginners. By the day of, they own the floor.
