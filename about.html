About Fajardo Elite.

A social dance academy where the dances of South Florida nights are taught by the people who actually mastered them — for the floor, not the trophy shelf.

At Fajardo Elite Dance Academy, we believe dance is a universal language and a transformative journey. Under the leadership of world-renowned instructors, we offer an unparalleled social-dance education for enthusiasts of every level — from the first time you cross the floor to the night you compete on it.

Our Founders

World champions Billy Fajardo & Leah Duque.

Fajardo Elite was brought to life by two of social dance's most decorated names. Together, Billy and Leah cultivate the environment where potential is nurtured and talent flourishes.

Our Approach

Tailored training for every dancer.

Whether you're taking your first steps onto the dance floor or honing your skills for a professional career, Fajardo Elite is your destination for world-class instruction. We specialize in:

  • Salsa — On1 and On2 patterns from beginner to performance team
  • Bachata — sensual and traditional, taught for the social floor
  • Latin Hustle — South Florida's signature partner dance
  • Cha-Cha & Merengue — through our Saturday Latin Rhythms class
  • Wedding Choreography — custom routines for your first dance
  • Private Lessons & Performance Teams — personalized development at every level

While private instruction is our specialty, we also offer engaging group classes for those who enjoy learning in a collaborative setting. Join us and experience the difference that truly elite social-dance instruction can make in your dance journey.
